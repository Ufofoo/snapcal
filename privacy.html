一、我们收集和处理的信息

本应用利用人工智能大语言模型，将您输入的自然语言文本转换为日历可读取的标准格式。

我们不会收集、存储或保留您的任何个人数据。具体而言：

  • 文本输入内容：您输入的文本仅用于实时调用 AI 模型进行格式转换，处理完成后即时丢弃，不会保存至任何服务器或数据库。
  • 账号信息：本应用无需注册或登录，不收集用户名、密码、手机号、邮箱等任何账号信息。
  • 设备信息：我们不会主动收集您的设备标识符、IP 地址、地理位置等信息。

二、第三方服务与数据传输

本应用可能涉及以下第三方服务：

  • AI 模型服务提供商：您输入的文本将被发送至中国大陆境内的第三方 AI 模型接口进行处理。该过程中，文本内容仅用于格式转换，我们不会向 AI 服务提供商发送任何可识别您身份的信息。
  • Apple 订阅服务：本应用的订阅付费通过 Apple 的应用内购买系统完成，相关交易信息由 Apple 根据其隐私政策处理，我们无法获取您的支付账户信息。

三、数据安全

尽管我们不存储您的数据，但在数据传输过程中，我们采用行业标准的加密措施（如 HTTPS/TLS）来保护您的文本内容在传输过程中的安全性。

四、儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 周岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 周岁以下儿童的任何信息。如果您是家长或监护人，发现您的孩子在未经同意的情况下使用了本应用，请联系我们。

五、您的权利

根据您所在地区的适用法律法规，您可能享有与个人信息相关的合法权利。

由于本应用不收集或存储任何个人信息，上述权利在实际操作中无需行使。如有疑问，仍可通过下方联系方式与我们联系。

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

ufofoo (hereinafter referred to as "we") understands the importance of your personal information. We protect your personal information and privacy in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Please read this policy carefully before using SnapCal (the "App").

1. Information We Collect and Process

The App uses AI large language models to convert your natural language text into a standard format readable by calendar apps.

We do NOT collect, store, or retain any of your personal data. Specifically:

  • Input Text: Text you enter is only used to call the AI model for real-time format conversion. It is discarded immediately after processing and is never saved to any server or database.
  • Account Information: The App does not require registration or login. We do not collect usernames, passwords, phone numbers, email addresses, or any account information.
  • Device Information: We do not actively collect device identifiers, IP addresses, location data, or other device-related information.

2. Third-Party Services & Data Transfer

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • AI Model Providers: Your input text is sent to third-party AI service providers located in Chinese mainland for processing. Text is used only for format conversion, and we do not send any personally identifiable information to AI providers.
  • Apple Subscriptions: In-app subscriptions are processed through Apple’s payment system. Transaction details are handled by Apple under its own privacy policy, and we cannot access your payment information.

3. Data Security

Although we do not store your data, we use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) during transmission to protect your text content.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App without consent, please contact us.

5. Your Rights

You may have legal rights regarding your personal information under applicable laws in your region.

Since the App does not collect or store personal data, these rights generally do not apply in practice. If you have questions, you may still contact us using the information below.
